The hexagonal prism of ℤ/2 × ℤ/6

Discriminant −231 has class group ℤ/2 × ℤ/6 — twelve curves, and non-cyclic, so no single prime's isogenies reach them all. It takes a set of two generators: the ℓ = 2 isogenies (a class of order 6) trace the two hexagons, and the ℓ = 3 isogenies (a class of order 2) are the rungs. Hover a vertex; press the mirror.

D = −231 = −3·7·11 · h = 12 · 4 real j-invariants (self-dual) · 4 complex-conjugate pairs
